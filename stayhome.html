1.Learn new recipes

CORNFLAKE COOKIES RECIPE (MAKE APPROXIMATELY 15 PCS) INGREDIENTS: - Flour 130 gr - 1 Egg - Sugar 80gr - Butter 125gr - 70gr cornflakes Bake it on 175 degree celcius for 15-20 minutes (depending on how big the cookies are and the oven)
Steps:
1. First add butter into a bowl. Next add Sugar.
2. Cream butter and sugar together until it appear light and fluffy.
3. Add and beat in egg.
4. Add flour and mix well.
5. Stir cornflakes and mix thoroughly.
6. Shape cookies and place on parchment-lined baking sheet.
7. After putting in the oven, check the colour of the cookie, the product should be baked to a golden brown crisp. You may probe it with a thin stick to check the consistency.

2. Make your own DIY Masks

Materials
-Two 25cm by 15cm cotton fabric
-Two 15cm piece of elastic band, rubber bands, head bands etc
-Needle and thread
-Scissors
-Sewing machine
Steps:
1. Use woven cotton, such as quilting fabric or cotton sheets or a T-shirt fabric.
2. Stack the two rectangles; you will sew the mask as if it was a single piece of fabric.
3. Fold over the long sides 0.6sm and hem.
4. Then fold the double layer of fabric over 0.6cm along the short sides and stitch down.
5. Run a 15cm length of 0.3cm wide elastic through the wider hem on each side of the mask.
6. These will be the ear loops. Use a large needle or a bobby pin to thread it through. Tie the ends tight.
7. Gently pull on the elastic so that the knots are tucked inside the hem.
8.Gather the sides of the mask on the elastic and adjust so the mask fits your face.
9. Then securely stitch the elastic in place to keep it from slipping.

4. Make your own DIY TieDie T-shirt

- One clean white shirt
- Dyes in bottles
- 4 Strong Elastic bands
- A container
- Gloves
Steps:
1. Lay your shirt flat. Begin twisting your shirt into a spiral shape, any shape you want to form into a disc shape.
2. Use strong elastic bands and tie to surround the shirt that has been formed into a disc shape.
3. (Wear gloves) Add dye and let the shirt absorb the dye. To make it simpler, fill it in into squirt bottles and decorate the shirt.
4. Leave it about a day and rinse off the dye.

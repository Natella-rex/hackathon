Tips To Protect Yourself

Wash your hand regularly with soap or use an alcohol-based sanitiser. By washing your hands and using hand sanitizers, you can kill viruses and bacteria on your hand. Click here to find out more Practice Social distancing.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-LKVUarhtvE

Stay at least one metre apart from others. This is because the virus can be spread through sneezing, coughing and talking through small liquid droplets. Hence, it is important to practice social distancing

Avoid crowded places. By being in the crowd, there is a higher chance of getting the virus due to close contact with other people

Do not touch your eyes, nose and mouth. Your hands might have come into contact with the virus when you touch surfaces. The virus can be transferred from your hand to your eyes, nose or mouth. Thus, do not touch them. If you need to, wash your hand before touching them.

Practice good respiratory hygenice. If you have a flu or a cough, use a tissue. This prevents possible spread of the virus and protects the people around you.

Stay home. Try minimising the time you go outside. Only go out if you need to. Eg. Buying groceries, seeing a doctor or buying your meals.

Seek medical attention IMMEDIATELY if you feel unwell. If you experience cough, fever and difficulty breathing, call your local health authority in advance and follow instructions from them. This allows them to be able to prepare for you the right health care facilities. It also alerts National and Local authorities so that they will have more accurate up to date information on the situations and prevents further spread of the virus

Stay well informed and keep up with the news from trusted sources such as WHO(world health Organisation) and national health authorities as they are able to advise you on what you can do to protect yourself
